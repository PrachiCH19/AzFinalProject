The word "organic" means the way farmers grow and process farming (agricultural) products. These products include fruits, vegetables, grains. Organic farming brings multiple benefits on the table which are both human health and earth friendly.Studies say Organic foods are extra rich with Nutrients, Vitamins,Omega-3 fatty acids and more. They also contain lesser of toxic metals. Cadmium is a toxic chemical naturally found in soils and absorbed by plants. It also reduces the usage of pesticides which is beneficial for both soil and Aquatic species.Soo, let's join hands and make "The Organic Store" part of every house in India.
